The challenge

The product was ready. The pipeline wasn't.

When Hal9 came to Limelight, they were preparing to launch VibeToContent — an AI-powered platform designed to help founders create content faster. The product was ready. The pipeline wasn't.

Their entire go-to-market relied on warm introductions, networking events, and word of mouth. They had no cold outbound infrastructure, no email sequences, no lead lists, and no systematic way to reach their target market at scale. Growth was entirely dependent on who they happened to meet.

They needed to generate qualified product demos to validate the launch and build early momentum. Limelight delivered the first result within 24 hours of campaigns going live.

The approach

Precision targeting from day one

Infrastructure built from the ground up

We architected and deployed a full cold email sending infrastructure — acquiring dedicated domains, configuring DNS authentication (SPF, DKIM, DMARC), and onboarding and warming a network of sending inboxes engineered for maximum deliverability. Every technical layer was built to land in primary inboxes, not spam folders.

Proprietary lead database — day one advantage

Rather than starting from scratch with list building, Limelight deployed a curated, pre-qualified list of founders directly from our internal database. We already knew which segment of the market would respond to an AI content offer — so campaigns went live with precision targeting on day one.

Offer testing & message-market fit

We developed multiple offer angles and copy variants, running structured tests to identify which positioning resonated with the market. Testing surfaced clear winners fast, and volume was concentrated behind the messages that were actually converting.

Built to scale, not to spike

The motion wasn't designed for a one-week burst. Warmed infrastructure, proven messaging, and a repeatable targeting playbook mean the campaign compounds — volume can scale without torching deliverability or reply rates.

The results

From zero to pipeline in 5 days

5 product demos booked in the first 5 days

Before Limelight, Hal9 had never run a single cold outbound campaign. Within 5 days of campaigns going live, 5 qualified founders had booked product demos for VibeToContent. No warm intros. No events. Pure outbound.

5 free trial signups — all actively using the product

Every demo converted to a free trial. All 5 founders signed up and began actively using VibeToContent within the same week campaigns launched. These weren't passive leads — they were engaged, product-active users.

1:150 emails per positive reply

Within the first 5 days of sending, Limelight achieved a positive reply rate of 1:150 — a strong benchmark for a brand-new campaign with no prior send history or domain reputation. As domains continue to warm and copy is refined, this number is expected to improve significantly.

Message-market fit confirmed

Testing multiple offer angles surfaced 1–2 winning messages that resonated clearly with the target market. This is the hardest variable to crack in cold outbound — and Limelight confirmed it in week one, giving Hal9 a repeatable, scalable messaging foundation.

What's next

Scaling a proven engine

Limelight is continuing to optimize the VibeToContent campaign with a clear target: 15–20 qualified demos per month. The levers are already in place — proven messaging, a warm sending infrastructure, and a playbook for what converts.

The next phase is scaling volume, layering in new offer variants, and systematically improving the email-to-reply ratio through continued A/B testing.

What started as zero outbound has become a repeatable pipeline engine — and it's just getting started.
